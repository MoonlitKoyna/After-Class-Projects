🌟 South Point School, Guwahati 🌟

🏫 About Us

Established in 1960, South Point School is a premier CBSE-affiliated co-educational institution located in Guwahati, Assam. With a commitment to academic excellence and all-round development, the school provides a nurturing environment where students can thrive.

🎯 Aim of Education

At South Point School, education isn't just about books and boards — it's about Holistic Development.Our aim is to empower students with knowledge, confidence, and compassion. We focus on intellectual development, moral values, discipline, creativity, and social responsibility. At South Point, we believe in shaping well-rounded individuals who are prepared for life's challenges.

📚 Academics

Follows CBSE curriculum
Classes from Pre-Primary to Class 12
Emphasis on conceptual understanding and analytical skills
Regular assessments, projects, and interactive learning

🎨 Co-Curricular Activities

Science and Computer laboratories
Well-stocked library and large auditorium
Boarding facilities for boys and girls
Safe and student-friendly environment
